Enchanting Table

Description

A magic table for enchanting things.

You can enchant tools or books. You will need lapis and experience levels.

Ingredients

  • 1 book
  • 2 diamonds
  • 4 obsidian blocks

Steps

  1. Use your crafting table
  2. Put the book in the middle of the first row
  3. In the second row, place a diamond, then an obsidian block, then the other diamond
  4. Place all the remaining obsidian blocks in the last row
  5. Press craft
  6. This will make one enchanting table
