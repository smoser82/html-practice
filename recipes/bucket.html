Bucket

Description

A metal bucket that you can put things in.

You can put water, lava, milk, or snow in it.

Ingredients

  • 3 iron ingots

Steps

  1. Use your crafting table
  2. Put one ingot in the middle of left column
  3. Put one ingot in the bottom of the middle column
  4. Put one ingot in the middle of the right column
  5. The ingots should be in a v shape
  6. Press craft
  7. You will get one bucket
